Terms and Conditions

MOXIE is a open source free social network which uses TOR to help facilitate a P2P network.

MOXIE was created by The Black Magic Project

The source code of MOXIE is available online and you can edit it however you want.

Data Usage

MOXIE does NOT record, store any personal data or give your data to any third parties.

Your data is stored locally on your device's memory. We do not store location data or any passwords. Due to the nature of MOXIE and it's strong adherence to privacy, we firmly believe your data belongs to you and anyone else you wish to share it with. This means MOXIE does not actively enforce content posted on the service. By using MOXIE, you will be held fully accountable by the content you post. Please make sure the content you post adhere's to your country's laws.

Safety Online

The general rule is do not post anything you wouldn't be happy to show your parents

We trust you are a good person and hope you take care on the platform.

Interactions between users of MOXIE are not monitored and considered to accountable between the parties involved.

If you are scammed due to the service, don't come crying to us. We can't help you.

Be polite, courteous and remember the internet rules

If you have any issues with MOXIE, please let us know on our dev forum or message us
