Know your rights

and anyone else you wish to share it with. This means MOXIE does not actively enforce content posted on the

service. By using MOXIE, you will be held fully accountable by the content you post. Please make sure the content

you post adhere's to your country's laws.

The general rule is do not post anything you wouldn't be happy to show your parents

We trust you are a good person and hope you take care on the platform.

Interactions between users of MOXIE are not monitored and considered to accountable between the parties involved.

If you are scammed due to the service, don't come crying to us. We can't help you.

Be polite, courteous and remember the internet rules

If you have any issues with MOXIE, please let us know on our dev forum or message us
